Welcome to DPlan+

Your customizable new tab page. Organize apps, RSS feeds, and more. Get started by configuring your homepage below!

How to set DPlan+ as your Chrome homepage:

  1. Click the button above to go to the configurator.
  2. Customize your apps, feeds, and theme.
  3. Copy the final URL generated.
  4. Open Chrome → Settings → On startup → Open a specific page or set of pages → Add a new page.
  5. Paste the copied DPlan+ URL as your homepage.
  6. Save and enjoy your personalized start page!
